Conflict Style Research · University Study

How do you handle conflict?

We're collecting data to study how people prefer to navigate disagreement — and to compare different ways of measuring that. This research is designed to improve the tools practitioners and organizations use when working with teams.

This study has two paid parts that take about 12–18 minutes in total. After completing them, you'll receive a completion code to submit on Prolific.

As a bonus, you'll have the option to view your personal conflict style profile and answer a few optional questions. This part is unpaid and completely voluntary.

What to expect: You'll answer a 20-item survey about how you approach conflict, followed by a second short assessment that uses a different format. There are no right or wrong answers — we're interested in your genuine tendencies.

By continuing, you consent to your anonymized responses being used for research. Your Prolific ID is stored only for payment purposes.
